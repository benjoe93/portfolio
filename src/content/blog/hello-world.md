For years I've pointed people to two places: ArtStation for the visual work and
GitHub for the code. As a technical artist that split never quite made sense —
most of what I do, like shaders and procedural tools, is *both* at once.

This site is the fix: one front door that showcases the whole discipline, links
back to ArtStation and GitHub (so their discoverability stays intact), and gives
me somewhere to actually write.
